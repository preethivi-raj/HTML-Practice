About Us

We have always been obsessed with pre-empting and fulfilling the wants of today diner. In 2012, it simply meant allowing diners to reserve a guaranteed table at the top restaurants in the city. In 2018, we has evolved to providing multiple benefits on one platform for all dining out needs.

Dineout was founded by Ankit Mehrotra, Nikhil Bakshi, Sahil Jain and Vivek Kapoor in 2012 and was acquired by Times Internet Limited in 2014. Times Internet is India largest digital products company and the digital venture of Times of India, India's largest media and entertainment group.
